## **1. Introduction** 

Dyafa Sales OS is a Hospitality Sales Management Platform designed to manage the complete sales lifecycle for Corporate Accounts, Corporate Agreements, Accommodation Reservations, Adhoc Sales Opportunities, Corporate Self-Service Booking, Sales Targets, Activities, Collections, AI Lead Generation, and Business Intelligence. 

The objective of the system is to provide a 360-degree view of every corporate client while improving the productivity of the Sales Team through automation, self-service capabilities, AI recommendations, and performance monitoring. 

## **2. Objectives**

- Manage Corporate Leads.

## **24. Executive Summary** 

Dyafa Sales OS is a Hospitality Sales Management Platform designed to unify Corporate Sales, Contract Management, Corporate Self-Service Booking, Adhoc Sales, Reservations, Collections, Targets, Activities, AI Lead Generation, and Business Intelligence into a single ecosystem. 

The platform will provide a 360-degree view of every corporate client throughout their complete lifecycle, beginning with AI-powered lead generation and continuing through contract signing, reservations, payments, renewals, and account growth. Corporate clients will benefit from self-service online booking capabilities at contracted rates, while the Sales Team will gain access to real-time dashboards, automated recommendations, performance analytics, and comprehensive KPI tracking. 

The solution will significantly improve sales productivity, customer experience, operational efficiency, and revenue generation while establishing a scalable foundation for future integrations and AI-driven hospitality sales capabilities.
